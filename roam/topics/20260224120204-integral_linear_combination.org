#+title: Integral Linear Combination
#+filetags: :topic:
* Definition
An *intergral linear combination* of \(a\) and \(b\) us an integer of the form \(ax+by\), where \(x,y \in \mathbb{Z}\).

* Key Points
- The set of all integral linear combinations of \(a\) and \(b\) is exactly the set of multiples of \(\gcd(a,b)\).
