#+title: Trigonometric ratios in right triangles
#+filetags: :topic:
* Key Points
- The ratios of the sides of a right angle triangle are called trigonometric ratios.

  Three common trigonometric ratios are:
  - *sine(sin)*
  - *cosine(cos)*
  - *tangent(tan)*

- The following are defined for an acute angle:
  - \(\sin(A) = \dfrac{\text{opposite}}{hypotenuse}\)

  - \(\cos(A) = \dfrac{\text{adjacent}}{\text{hypotenuse}}\)

  - \(\tan(A)=\dfrac{\text{opposite}}{\text{adjacent}}\)
