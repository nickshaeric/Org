#+title: Pythagorean identity
#+filetags: :topic:
* Definition
Pythagorean Identity: \({\sin{(\theta)}}^2+{\cos{(\theta)}}^2=1\)

* Key Points
The identity is used for all real values of \(\theta\).
It is a result of applying the Pythagorean theorem on the right triangle that is formed in the unit circle for each $\theta$.
