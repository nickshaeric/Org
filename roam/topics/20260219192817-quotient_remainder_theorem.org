#+title: Quotient-Remainder Theorem
#+filetags: :topic:
* Definition
** Quotient–Remainder Theorem
Theorem: For all integers $n$ and $d$, where $d > 0$, there exist *unique* integers $q$ and $r$ such that $n = qd + r \quad \text{and} \quad 0 \le r < d$.

Notation: $n \bmod d$

- $q$ = quotient when $n$ is divided by $d$
- $r$ = remainder when $n$ is divided by $d$
